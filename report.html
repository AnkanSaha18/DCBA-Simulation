What is DCBA?

A dual-chain blockchain system solving counterfeit pharmaceuticals and last-mile delivery simultaneously

DCBA addresses a critical gap: no existing system combines pharmaceutical supply chain integrity with UAV delivery logistics on separate, purpose-optimized blockchains. The PDC uses Ethereum PoA for strong patient data privacy; the UOC uses Hyperledger Fabric for high-throughput IoT operations. SC-7 bridges them with replay-safe prescription verification.

Why two chains? Patient data demands privacy and permissioned access (PDC). UAV telemetry demands throughput and IoT-optimized semantics (UOC). No single blockchain satisfies both simultaneously — hence the dual-chain design.
